About

Rooted in flavor, served with warmth

Urban Bistro blends modern cuisine with neighborhood comfort. Our chefs craft seasonal plates with responsibly sourced ingredients, while the front-of-house team keeps the atmosphere inviting, relaxed, and ready for lingering conversations.

Locally sourced produce and premium proteins.

Handmade sauces, doughs, and desserts made daily.

Warm service, curated wine list, and craft cocktails.
